User Data Deletion Policy

This document outlines the process and policy regarding user data deletion from Leerai.co.za, as required by Facebook's developer guidelines.

Data Deletion Instructions:

If you wish to delete your data from our application, you can remove your information by following these steps:

  1. Go to your Facebook account's Settings & Privacy.
  2. Click on Settings.
  3. Navigate to Apps and Websites.
  4. Select our application (Leerai) from the list.
  5. Click Remove to revoke permissions.

Alternative Method:

If you require further assistance or prefer manual deletion of your data, please contact us directly:

  • Email: admin@leerai.co.za

Please specify your Facebook user ID or the email address associated with your account clearly in your request. We will process your data deletion request within 30 days and send you a confirmation email once your data has been permanently deleted.

Data Deletion Confirmation:

Upon completion of the deletion process, all personal information linked to your account, retrieved through Facebook, will be permanently erased from our servers and cannot be recovered.
